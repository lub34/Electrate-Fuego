Field or Career

The systems I will hopefully go on to work with should focus on returning a part of the system if not all of it to a desired state -- an arbitrary equilibrium. However, during my education, I've also learned about equilibrium on a more personal level. College has forced me to make time for myself -- whether it's the gym, eating healthy, getting enough sleep, etc. If I focus too much on my studies and avoid these, I lose my equilibrium. And I very well have lost it at times. But thanks to these trials, I've learned to value equilibrium and, while my desired career revolves around it in an entirely different light, my hope is to help the world by making it easier for equilibrium to be maintained in various machines and other parts of our future life.
